Fallow - Work in Progress

Interactive / Asset Design

Fallow is a mental health app currently in testing that turns daily self-care into a quiet, rewarding ritual.

Users track small, positive daily activities—like getting outside, completing tasks, or practicing mindfulness. Each healthy action earns in-game currency, which can be used to buy fantasy plants for a personal garden.

The garden grows with you.

Plants don’t just appear fully formed; they evolve over time based on consistency and habit strength. Showing up regularly helps plants grow taller, bloom, or unlock rare variations, visually reflecting progress without pressure or streak anxiety.

Fallow is designed to feel calm, slow, and non-judgmental.

No punishments for missed days. No productivity guilt. Just gentle encouragement through growth, care, and play.

Prototype releasing on January 6th, 2026.
