BrainGauge

MedDesign / AI

Braingauge is a monitoring app for contact-sport athletes that establishes a cognitive baseline in week one by recording speech patterns, eye movement, reaction time, and memory. Users repeat the same short assessments weekly, allowing the app to track subtle changes over time. By visualizing trends rather than single scores, Braingauge helps flag potential early signs associated with CTE and cumulative head trauma, giving athletes and clinicians data to support informed decisions about training, recovery, and long-term brain health. Current version requires users to provide their own WhisperAPI key.

Built with team of two + ChatGPT to assist in implementing backend.
